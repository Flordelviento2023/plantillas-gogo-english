👀 Look & Notice
Sentence A: "Hello there. I'm Mia." (to Tom, in the office)

Sentence B: "Hi. I'm Mia Torres, from the Design Team." (to the team, with the boss)
Which sentence is more formal?
¿Qué oración es más formal?
Sentence B is more formal. Mia uses her full name because she's talking to her boss, not just a coworker.
👀 Look & Notice
Look at Sentence B again: "Hi. I'm Mia Torres, from the Design Team." What extra information does Mia give here, that she didn't give in Sentence A?
Mira la Oración B otra vez. ¿Qué información extra da Mia aquí, que no dio en la Oración A?
Her full name (not just "Mia") and her team/department. We usually add this information in formal introductions.
